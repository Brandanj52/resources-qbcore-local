This is the 2018 Dodge Durango SRT by Jrem7315 & GTA Graphical

Screenshots are by Abhi
this model came from Forza Motorsport 7, and has a few bugs that will be updated very shortly.

model has working doors, real life scale, correct wheel size, engine model.
known bugs are licence plates, needs corect lighing for headlights and taillights, and gauges among others.

Add On and Replace.

INSTALLATION: replace with what you want until i get default replace file. right click on model you want to replace
and click and replace with yft and ytd files accordingly.
Add-On: Spawn name: durango. create folder in mods/update/x64/dlcpacks, name it "durango" then go to
 mods/update/update/common/data/dlclist and replace with this line <item>dlcpacks:\durango\</item>
for best experience i recommend changing handling line and vehicle meta line to ones below
